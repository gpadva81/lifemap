🧩 New Modules

🧭 Values & Vision Discovery Front End

Guided exercises from the Passion & Purpose Clarity Workshop — interactive, step-by-step.

  • ✦ Core Values Ranking — drag & rank your top 10 from 50+ values
  • ✦ "What Makes Time Stop?" — passion identification prompts
  • ✦ Ideal Day Visualization — write your perfect day in detail
  • ✦ Legacy Statement — "What do I want to be remembered for?"
  • ✦ Life Wheel Assessment — rate 8 life areas, see imbalance
  • ✦ "I Am" Statement Builder — generates your affirmations
🎯 Goal Cascade Engine Front End

Distill your big vision into actionable weekly tasks using structured goal decomposition.

  • ✦ 3-Year Vision Board — big dreams per life category (Health, Wealth, Love, Happiness)
  • ✦ 1-Year Milestones — measurable targets with deadlines
  • ✦ 90-Day Rocks — quarterly focus areas (Scaling Up methodology)
  • ✦ Weekly Auto-Populate — 90-day rocks feed into weekly priorities automatically
  • ✦ SMART Goal Validator — checks each goal for specificity & measurability
  • ✦ Goal Alignment View — visual tree connecting weekly actions to life vision
📊 Habit Analytics Dashboard Back End

Statistical tracking of habit execution across weeks, months, and quarters.

  • 📈 Weekly Habit Completion Rate — bar chart per week
  • 🗓️ Habit Heatmap — GitHub-style calendar showing daily consistency
  • 📉 Trend Lines — rolling averages, best/worst weeks
  • 🔍 Per-Habit Drill-Down — which habits you nail vs. which slip
  • 📅 Day-of-Week Patterns — "You're weakest on Sundays"
  • 🎯 Goal vs. Actual — planned priorities vs. completed
📉 Goal Progress Tracker Back End

Track 90-day rocks and 1-year milestones with measurable progress indicators.

  • 📊 Progress Rings — visual % complete per goal
  • 🏁 Milestone Timeline — Gantt-style view of goal deadlines
  • ⚡ Velocity Tracking — are you on pace to hit your target?
  • 🔔 At-Risk Alerts — goals falling behind get flagged
  • 📝 Weekly Reflection Logs — journal entries tied to goal progress
  • 🔗 Category Breakdown — Health / Wealth / Love / Happiness scores
🏆 Gamification Engine Gamify

Reward consistency and execution — make the process addictive.

  • 🔥 Streak Counter — consecutive days/weeks of full completion
  • ⭐ XP & Levels — earn points for habit completion, goal progress
  • 🏅 Achievement Badges — "Early Riser" (30 days of 5:30 AM), "Ironman" (100% habits for a week)
  • 📈 Weekly Score Leaderboard — compete against your own past weeks
  • 🎁 Milestone Celebrations — confetti/animations when hitting big goals
  • 💎 Life Map Level — Beginner → Committed → Master → Legend
🤖 AI Accountability Coach Gamify

Personalized insights and nudges based on your patterns — that's where I come in.

  • 💬 Daily Morning Brief — today's priorities + affirmation
  • 📊 Weekly Performance Review — "You hit 87% this week, up from 72%"
  • ⚠️ Pattern Alerts — "You've skipped meditation 3 days in a row"
  • 💡 Smart Suggestions — "Try pairing meditation with your morning coffee"
  • 🏆 Celebration Messages — acknowledge wins and streaks
  • 📅 Sunday Planning Prompt — guided weekly planning session
